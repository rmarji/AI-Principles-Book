### **Subtitle:** *The Leader's Guide to Leveraging Artificial Intelligence and AI Agents for Peak Performance*

---

### **BOOK OVERVIEW**

A practical playbook for leaders and professionals to strategically integrate AI and autonomous AI agents into their workflows, build AI agent teams, and design AI-powered organizational structures. This book shows how to move beyond simple AI tools to orchestrating networks of AI agents that work together—creating your personal "AI workforce" and revolutionizing team productivity.
