## Key Lessons Across Cases

### Success Factors

| Factor | Evidence Across Cases |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Clear Use Case Definition** | Most failures traced to vague objectives |
| **Human-AI Collaboration Design** | Best results combine AI efficiency with human judgment |
| **Quality Feedback Loops** | Continuous improvement requires measurement |
| **Change Management** | Technical success often fails without adoption |
| **Executive Sponsorship** | Scale requires visible leadership support |

### Common Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Frequency | Prevention |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Over-automation | High | Start with augmentation, not replacement |
| Insufficient Training | High | Invest in user enablement |
| Ignoring Edge Cases | Medium | Test thoroughly before launch |
| Unclear Ownership | Medium | Assign accountability explicitly |
| Scaling Too Fast | Medium | Prove value before expanding |
